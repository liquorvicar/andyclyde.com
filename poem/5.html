today tomorrow next week

of milk-float nights
and of days
with meteor-storms eluding
it's last night's sky
laden with best-laid plans
life running against the blur of days
to find a time where land forgot

it's tomorrow's sky
and it cries sweet memories

today i forgot
how to walk
and how to breathe
and how to breathe
and walk.
