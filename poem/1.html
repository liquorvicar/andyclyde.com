old bean

i made some coffee.
it laughed and so i let it die
and when its skin was wrinkled
and its memory had gone,
i put it in a home.
we even went to visit,
once or twice,
the sugarcubes and i,
but then it spilt its beans.
or so the warden said,
'its gone to meet its maker.'
me.
